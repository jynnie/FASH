Rules

FASH is a new social event created to promote family adventures! Hang out with your family, meet new people, and WIN PRIZES!

Complete the missions to earn points! In order to receive your points, submit picture proof online.

Missions must be completed with 1+ other ATS members. An extra point is awarded if you bring along a new member! Starred missions must be done with at least five members of your family (including yourself) together in order to qualify.

Individual prizes are awarded depending on the amount of points you get. Families compete against each other, and in the end, the family with the most points will get a prize (TBD).

Go out there and get exploring!
